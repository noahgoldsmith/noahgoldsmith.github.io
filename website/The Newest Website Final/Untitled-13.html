Hi everyone,

My name is Noah Goldsmith, I am a Mass Comm Major/Business Minor.

In this class I hope to learn some tools that will help me with my brand.

I’m from Edwardsville Illinois. I graduated during COVID from Edwardsville High School in 2020 and decided to continue my athletic and academic career at Millikin University.

I never visited the school before, but it felt like the right thing to do so I went on faith.

I’m a church boy without a doubt and never had that much freedom before so I decided to act a fool.

After an epiphany I decided it was time to come back home after the first year.

I hung up the cleats, cleaned up my act and I am doing much better.

Now, I work as a bank teller while pursuing my degree. In my spare time, I play several instruments, do theatrical production, produce, and release music, host events, and build my brand “Blessup Gear.”

I created Blessup Gear in 2017 as a sophomore in high school by selling clothes out my bookbag, to out my trunk, to eventually a store. Blessup Gear has provided me an outlet to share my gifts with the world. If you guys are interested follow the Instagram below! #BLESSUP
